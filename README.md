Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard (Power BI)
 Overview
The Madhav Ecommerce Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and visualize ecommerce business performance. It provides insights into sales, profit, customer behavior, product categories, and payment methods, helping stakeholders make informed, data-driven decisions.

Objectives
Monitor overall sales, profit, and quantity
Analyze monthly profit trends
Identify top-performing states and customers
Understand product category performance
Evaluate customer payment preferences
Support business growth through insights

 Key Metrics
Total Sales Amount: 255
Total Quantity Sold: 376
Total Profit: 163
Average Order Value (AOV): 33K

 Features
 Sales Overview
Displays key KPIs: Sales Amount, Quantity, Profit, and AOV
Provides a quick summary of overall performance
 Profit Analysis
Monthly profit visualization
Helps identify trends and seasonal performance
 State-wise Sales Analysis
Sales distribution across states (Karnataka, Kerala, etc.)
Identifies top-performing regions

Customer Analysis
Sales contribution by individual customers
Helps identify high-value customers
Category Analysis
Quantity distribution across categories:
Clothing (highest share)
Electronics
Furniture

 Sub-Category Profit
Profit analysis for specific products (e.g., Saree, Stole, Handkerchief)
Helps in product-level decision-making
Payment Method Analysis
Distribution of transactions across:
COD (Cash on Delivery)
UPI
EMI
Credit Card
Helps understand customer payment preferences

Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
DAX – Measures and calculations
Data Modeling – Relationship building

Dataset

Ecommerce sales dataset
Includes:
Orders
Customers
Products
Payment details

How to Use

1. Download the .pbix file
2. Open in Power BI Desktop
3. Explore visuals using filters and slicers
4. Analyze insights interactively
